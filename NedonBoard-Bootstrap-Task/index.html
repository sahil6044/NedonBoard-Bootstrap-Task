about us

Developing strong, effective, and forward-thinking non-executives at the highest leve

As the Institute of Board Members, everything we do at NEDonBoard revolves around bringing the very best support to every stage of your non-executive career, from securing your first appointment through to ongoing continuous professional development, expanding your portfolio, and joining a thriving community as an established and successful non-executive director or board member. Our certified training for non-executive and board directors, underpinned by the Institute of Board Members is helping non-executive directors to lead, challenge and support the strategic direction of organisations and instil world-class business governance practices in today’s rigorous and fast-moving operating environments.
